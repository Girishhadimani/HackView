How DeepShield Works

01
🧬

SRM + FFT Analysis

Steganalysis Rich Model filters extract manipulation noise residuals invisible to the human eye. 2D-FFT detects GAN spectral fingerprints.

02
🧠

EfficientNet-B4 Backbone

Spatial feature extraction via pre-trained EfficientNet-B4, fine-tuned on FaceForensics++ and DFDC datasets.

03
⏱

TimeSformer Temporal

Divided Space-Time Attention transformer detects frame-to-frame flickering and lip-sync mismatches (Wav2Lip attacks).

04
🎙

wav2vec2 Audio

Fine-tuned wav2vec 2.0 + Mel spectrogram CNN detects voice cloning, TTS synthesis, and unnatural prosody patterns.

05
🎯

Ensemble Fusion

GBM + MLP meta-learner fuses all modality signals with EXIF metadata for the final authenticity score.

06
📊

Grad-CAM Explanation

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Maps highlight the exact image regions that triggered the deepfake verdict.
